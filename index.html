3 Key Steps to Investing in the Caribbean

Learn about the simplified legal and financial process for foreigners.

1. Evaluation and Reservation

We start with personalized advice to align the property with your investment goals (ROI or lifestyle). When choosing your ideal unit, we formalize the interest with a Purchase Offer and a 100% refundable reservation deposit. This freezes the price, removes the unit from the market, and gives us time to review the legal documentation without risk to you.

2. Trust and Formalities

We manage the creation of your Bank Trust, the legal instrument that grants you full property rights (to sell, inherit, or rent) as a foreigner. Our team coordinates with the Public Notary to investigate the title and ensure the property is free of liens, as well as process the permits before the Secretaría de Relaciones Exteriores (SRE).

3. Closing and Delivery

The final step is the signing of the Public Deed before a Notary and the settlement of the remaining balance. We not only deliver the keys; we also conduct a detailed physical walkthrough to ensure everything is perfect. Finally, we connect you with our rental management partners so your investment starts generating income from the first day.
