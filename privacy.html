Last updated: April 2026

1. Identity and Address of the Data Controller

TuCasa Caribe Realty (hereinafter, "The Data Controller"), located at Puerto Morelos Quintana Roo , is responsible for the processing of personal data collected through our website and other contact methods.

2. Personal Data Collected

We collect the following personal data from our users through contact forms and email:

  • Identification Data: Full name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number (WhatsApp).
  • Interest Data: Type of property of interest, specific message or request.
  • Browsing Data (Cookies): Technical information about website usage (see section 5).

3. Purposes of Data Processing

The personal data collected will be used for the following primary and necessary purposes for our service:

  • Contact: To respond to information requests, quotes, and inquiries regarding properties for sale or rent.
  • Real Estate Advisory: To provide personalized advice, send property listings and virtual tours, and manage procedures related to the property of interest.
  • Commercial Follow-up: To maintain contact during the buying/renting process and subsequent follow-up.

4. Data Transfer and Security

Your personal data will not be transferred to third parties without your express consent, except for exceptions provided by law, such as legal requirements or court orders.

The Data Controller is committed to protecting your personal data through administrative, technical, and physical security measures to prevent damage, loss, alteration, destruction, or unauthorized use, access, or processing.

By entering our website, you accept the use of non-essential cookies. In compliance with regulations, a banner will be displayed to request your explicit consent.
